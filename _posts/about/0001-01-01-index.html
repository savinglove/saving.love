About saving.love

Preaching I'm a sinner is different than preaching to disobey and/or do evil.
saving.love makes sure I practice what I preach to these measures below:

  • I disobey God... I'm a human sinner, it's a practice (every split second).
    I preach I'm unable to stop being a sinner who makes evil mistakes in God's eyes.
  • I preach I'm not perfect.
    I preach I disobey God's demands to be perfect.
    I practice what I preach because God demands to be perfect, and I'm not perfect.
  • I preach I mostly don't know the infinitely powerful and knowledged Trinity (the one true God) using God's knowledge defined... compared to mine. 
    Our knowledge is: we know him if we keep/obey his commandments. (1 John 2:3)

saving.love doesn't share if others do or don't deserve Hell and/or the Lake of Fire.
I share I'm deserving of Hell and the Lake of Fire (forever).

  • Belief about Hell and the Lake of Fire isn't required to become saved.


In the end, I believe we'll all be judged by the measures and methods we judge others.
This website doesn't preach whether or not to judge others.
